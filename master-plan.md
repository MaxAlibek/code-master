# Master Plan: Pro Learning Platform (DRM Edition) - v3.0 "Cupertino Midnight"

## 1. Engineering & Design Identity
- **Design System:** Cupertino Midnight (Absolute Black **#000000**, High-Gloss Glassmorphism, 20px radius).
- **Core Principle:** "Premium Engineering Experience" (Minimalism, Inter font, 1px subtle borders).
- **Theme Engine:** Global persistent theme (Light/Dark) with **100% coverage**. Switching updates the entire DOM instantly via `html.dark`.

## 2. Feature: IDE Sync (Deep Linking)
- **Protocol:** `vscode://file/{path}`.
- **Dynamic Pathing:** `project/practice/{track}/{lesson_id}/task_{n}.{ext}`.

## 3. Advanced Learning & Track Isolation
- **Tracks:** Frontend, Backend, Mobile, Data Science (All unlocked).
- **Dynamic Content:** AI-generated theory and tasks via Gemini API.

## 4. AI Brain & Localization (The "Pure Locale" Engine)
- **Multi-language:** 100% UI and Content coverage for **EN, RU, and UZ**.
- **Live Translation:** AI-Mentor feedback, Theory, and Practice Tasks are generated in the user's active language (`{lang}`).
- **Anti-Cheat:** Zero repetition in assessments; adaptive difficulty per session.

## 5. Mastery-Based Progression (The 4/5 Rule)
- **Strict Gate:** Next lesson stays **LOCKED** until current avg score >= 4.0 stars.
- **Visuals:** Apple-style progress rings and dynamic status indicators.

## 6. AI Senior Mentor (DRM Lead Persona)
- **Context:** Acts as a "DRM Senior Lead Engineer".
- **PARA Sync:** Automated terminal logs showing resource sync into Projects/Resources folders after verification.
- **UI:** High-gloss glass sidebar with **backdrop-filter: blur(25px)** and GitHub-style review comments.

## 7. Engineering Analytics (Cupertino Style)
- **Visuals:** Minimalist Recharts Skill Growth + GitHub-style Activity Heatmap.
- **Widgets:** Glass cards for "Avg Code Quality", "Lines Verified", and "Active Deadlines".

## 8. UX & Feedback
- **Confetti:** Explosive multi-origin bursts for 5-star completions.
- **Terminal:** Realistic log-terminal simulation during AI verification (Scanning -> Linting -> AI Review).

## 9. Gamification (Evolutionary UI)
- **Dynamic Badges:** Track levels evolve (Beginner -> Master) with dynamic colors (Green -> Blue -> Purple -> Gold).
- **Logic:** Level calculation based on `% Completion` + `Avg AI Score`.

## 10. Interactive Demos (Landing Page)
- **Feature Cards:** Hover-activated mini-demos for IDE Sync, AI Mentor, and PARA.
- **Visuals:** Pure Apple aesthetic with fluid transitions and high-contrast text.
